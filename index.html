PCS Express — Military Relocation Readiness

PCS Express helps service members and military families simplify Permanent Change of Station (PCS) moves through guided workflows, centralized relocation tools, and military-specific resources. Built for all DoD branches, all components (Active Duty, Reserve, Guard, Civilian), and military families.

Who we serve

  • Active Duty — All branches and ranks, from junior enlisted to flag officer.
  • Reserve Component — Title 10 mobilizations, annual training relocations, and PCS-equivalent moves.
  • National Guard — Title 32 activations, AGR, and federal-active orders.
  • Military Spouses — Family-side checklists, license portability, and SECO career resources.
  • Veterans Transitioning — Post-separation relocation, GI Bill schools, VA benefits, and SCRA protections.
  • DoD Civilians — GS, SES, and WG employees with FTR §302 relocation, LQA, and TQSA workflows.
  • First-time PCS Families — Step-by-step guidance for the very first move — what to ask, when to ask it.
  • CONUS & OCONUS — Stateside moves and overseas PCS with pet-import, country-clearance, and EFMP support.

Features

BAH and OHA calculators, LQA and TQSA workflows, PPM (personally procured move) trackers, weight-allowance estimators, household-goods shipment timelines, in-transit storage policies, partial DITY guidance, allowance comparisons, base-finder tools, school-rating overlays, neighborhood-safety integrations, SCRA-protection checklists, EFMP-family planning, country-clearance workflows for OCONUS moves, and a step-by-step 90-day (CONUS) / 180-day (OCONUS) timeline that adjusts to the user's orders.

How it works

Create a free profile, paste the highlights of your orders, and PCS Express assembles a tailored relocation plan: a date-ordered task timeline, the right calculators for your component (Active / Reserve / Guard / Civilian), the right allowances for your destination (CONUS BAH, OCONUS OHA, civilian LQA), and a side-by-side comparison of your move options (full government move vs. PPM vs. partial DITY). The plan updates as you complete tasks and reflects real DoD policy windows (e.g., the 60-day pre-move government move request window for OCONUS).

Security & privacy

Profile data stays on your device by default (AES-256-GCM at rest in browser storage). HTTPS-only transport. No document uploads, no demographic data collection, no third-party advertising trackers beyond the disclosed AdSense relationship, no session-replay vendors. We disclose every third-party service we route data to in our privacy notice, and explain every DoD-specific calculation in our methodology footnotes.
