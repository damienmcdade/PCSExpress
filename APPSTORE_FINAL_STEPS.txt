================================================================================
📱 PCS EXPRESS - FINAL STEPS TO APP STORE
================================================================================

Your iOS app is READY TO SUBMIT! Follow these 3 simple steps:

================================================================================
STEP 1: PREREQUISITES (5 minutes)
================================================================================

1. Sign up for Apple Developer ($99/year)
   https://developer.apple.com/account

2. Create app in App Store Connect
   https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
   
   - Click "My Apps" → "+" → "New App"
   - Platform: iOS
   - Name: PCS Express
   - Bundle ID: com.pcsexpress.app
   - SKU: pcsexpress-v1

3. Verify Xcode is installed on your Mac
   xcodebuild -version

================================================================================
STEP 2: RUN AUTOMATED SUBMISSION (10 minutes)
================================================================================

On your Mac, run:

   cd ~/path/to/pcs-express
   bash submit-to-appstore.sh

This script will:
  ✓ Build your web app
  ✓ Sync to iOS
  ✓ Create archive
  ✓ Validate app
  ✓ Upload to App Store
  ✓ Print success message

When prompted: Enter your Apple ID (password NOT stored)

================================================================================
STEP 3: SUBMIT FOR REVIEW (15 minutes)
================================================================================

After upload completes:

1. Go to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. Click "Your App" → "Builds"
3. Wait for build to appear (5-10 min)
4. Select your build
5. Add metadata:
   - Screenshots (2-5, size 1242x2208)
   - Description
   - Keywords: military, PCS, relocation, AI
   - Support URL
   - Privacy Policy URL
   - Rating (Content Rating form)
6. Click "Submit for Review" (blue button)

================================================================================
DONE!

Your app is now:
  ✅ In review at Apple (1-5 days)
  ✅ Waiting for approval
  ✅ On track to be in App Store

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: "xcrun: command not found"
Solution: xcode-select --install

Problem: "Archive creation failed"
Solution: rm -rf build && bash submit-to-appstore.sh

Problem: "Signing certificate error"
Solution: open ios/App/App.xcworkspace → General → Sign In
