FAQ

What is PCS?

PCS stands for Permanent Change of Station — a military transfer to a new duty location.

How do I use PCS Express?

Enter your questions about PCS moves, base assignments, or service member transitions. Our AI provides personalized guidance.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and do not store your queries. HTTPS protects all communications.

Can I get help with specific bases?

Ask about any base or duty location. Our AI provides current information and service member experiences.
