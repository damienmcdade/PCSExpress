PCS Express Icon Concepts

Five professional, service-neutral directions inspired by PCS moves, orders, duty stations, household goods, family readiness, and mission planning.

Source material

The current icon centers on family relocation, a road/path, boxes, and a home destination. These options preserve that core promise while adding a more military-oriented command, readiness, and duty-station feel.

1. Mission Route Crest

Strongest military posture: shield, route, rank-like bars, and home/move readiness.

2. Orders Compass

Best for “orders to action”: compass navigation plus official paperwork.

3. Convoy to Home

Most literal PCS move: HHG shipment, road movement, and destination home.

4. Family Readiness

Closest to the current family-centered icon, but more formal and official.

5. Global Duty Station

Best for CONUS/OCONUS scope: installation locator, global moves, and navigation.
